How Cell Tower and Wi-Fi triangulation help in tracking location?

Location-based services that provides the actual coordinates of the Cell Towers, Hotspot and Company Address etc. The technology of locating is based on various complex algorithms and authenticated data set.

The fact that there are more Wi-Fi hotspots than trees in a city enables Google or Apple to identify your location by merely knowing the strength of Wi-Fi signals on your phone. This data is available for app developers through APIs which any developer can leverage to locate phones using Wi-Fi positioning.
